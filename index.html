About Us

At Luciana's Mexican Restaurant and Cantina we are committed to using top quality, fresh ingredients to create authentic Mexican cuisine. Our specialties include: sizzling fajitas, cheesy arroz con pollo, and delicious frozen margaritas.
Luciana's Mexican Restaurant and Cantina is the fulfillment of two foodies' dreams. Once upon a time a Gringa fell in love with a Mexicano and dreamed big dreams, that quickly became realities. First they had each other, then they were blessed with a little princess, Luciana. Affectionately named after their beautiful daughter, Luciana's Mexican Restaurant and Cantina is a place where families come together, dream big dreams, and enjoy delicious comida!
